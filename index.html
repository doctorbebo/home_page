My name is Byron Brown and my friends call me Bebo. Growing up on a small dairy farm in a little town in central PA has taught me the value of a hard work and integrity. Though I love my hometown and it holds a special place in my heart, I had to explore the world. I love technology and rain so the decision to move to Seattle was simple.

I grew up always fascinated with technology. I remember watching older cousins playing video games and simply being enthralled. This fascination grew into a desire to replicate the feelings and emotions of a great user experience. I am finishing a program in web development where I have mastered HTML, CSS, jQuery, node.js and React.js

I love Seattle's tech culture. That was one of the reasons I moved here in the first place. Through meetups and hackathons I have gotten to know the tech culture well and hope to establish an exciting career in the greater Seattle area.
